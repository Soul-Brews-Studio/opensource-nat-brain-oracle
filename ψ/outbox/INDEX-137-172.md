## Generation Guide

### Thai Text Specifications
All images feature centered Thai text as prominent visual element:
- **Font**: Clean, modern, readable (not decorative)
- **Size**: Large and clear, visible from distance
- **Color**: Luminous/glowing (often white, gold, or matching dominant theme color)
- **Position**: Centered or integrated with composition
- **Effect**: Glow, shadow, or integration into the image

### Visual Style Parameters
All prompts include:
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (horizontal/widescreen)
- **Format**: PNG preferred
- **Quality**: High detail, professional finish
- **Composition**: Balanced, intentional, symbolic
- **Color Depth**: Vibrant, meaningful color choices

- Leonardo AI (best Thai text rendering)
- Midjourney (excellent artistic quality)
