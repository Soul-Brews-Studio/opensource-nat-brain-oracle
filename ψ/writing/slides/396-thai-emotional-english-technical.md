# Thai When Emotional, English When Technical

## Thai Text
ภาษาไทยตอนรู้สึก ภาษาอังกฤษตอนคิด - สองภาษา สองโลก
(Phasa Thai tohn ru seuk, Phasa Angkrit tohn kit - Song phasa song lok)

## Prompt
Split-screen monochrome ink-wash composition. Left side: figures speaking, warm watercolor wash, flowing Thai script floating in air (depicted as organic curves and circles), emotional gestures with open hands, hearts visible in watercolor. Golden light warm and embracing. Right side: same figures but analytical, cyan geometric patterns, English characters forming structured grids and algorithms, precise hand gestures (pointing, measuring), cool light. Zero gravity gently swirls both sides, maintaining separation yet showing they're part of whole. Center border shows flowing transition (not hard line)—emotions giving way to precision. Oracle style honors both modes as equally valuable. Ink wash background unifies both worlds. Visual language: curves meet angles, flow meets structure.
