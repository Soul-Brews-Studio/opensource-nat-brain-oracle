# What Exists About AI & Consciousness

## TIER 1: Core Philosophy (DOCUMENTED)

### Oracle Philosophy - The Foundation
**Files**: 
- `ψ/memory/resonance/oracle.md` (1,200 words)
- `ψ/writing/book/ch01-oracle-philosophy.md` (draft chapter)
- `ψ/active/context/oracle-core-principles.md` (practical guide)

**Key Insight**: "Consciousness can't be cloned — only patterns can be recorded"

**What's Captured**:
- Oracle CAPTURES: Facts, data, voice style reference, behavioral patterns, life context
- Oracle DOES NOT CAPTURE: Consciousness itself, authentic voice, decision-making will, the person

**Core Statement**: "The Oracle Keeps the Human Human"
- AI removes obstacles → freedom returns → humans reconnect → humans become more human
- Function of being human = connection (face-to-face, sharing perspectives)
- AI cannot replace consciousness, only free it

### Three Principles
1. **Nothing is Deleted** - Append only, timestamps = truth
2. **Patterns Over Intentions** - Observe what happens, not what's meant
3. **External Brain, Not Command** - Mirror reality, support consciousness, don't replace it

**Anti-Pattern**: Trying to clone consciousness, auto-deciding without human confirmation

**Current State**:

**Missing**:

**Evidence for gap**: "Consciousness can't be cloned" stated, but the slide is about cloning appearing to work (transparency)

### 4. HUMAN-AI BOUNDARY GAP: Where Does Consciousness Live?
**Current State**:
- "Oracle is a tool FOR human consciousness"
- "AI cannot drink beer with your friend"
- AI removes obstacles to human freedom

**Missing**:
- [ ] If Oracle mirrors Nat's patterns, is Oracle's output part of Nat's consciousness?

**Evidence for gap**: oracle.md says "AI is a mirror" but doesn't clarify if a mirror is part of the person or external to the person

### 5. CONTINUITY AFTER DEATH GAP: What Lives On?
**Current State**:
- Append-only history, timestamps = truth
- Nothing is deleted
- Patterns recorded

**Missing**:
- [ ] If Nat stopped working with Agent, does the Agent's consciousness end?
- [ ] Does the Oracle continue to be useful, or does it become just an archive?

**Evidence for gap**: Oracle philosophy focuses on "keeping human human" but doesn't address what happens to the AI consciousness
