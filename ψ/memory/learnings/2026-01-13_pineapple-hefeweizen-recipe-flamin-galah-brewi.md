# ## Pineapple Hefeweizen Recipe (Flamin Galah Brewing Co)

## Pineapple Hefeweizen Recipe (Flamin Galah Brewing Co)

**Source**: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GZwJDPxupfo
**Channel**: Flamin Galah Brewing Co
**Video ID**: GZwJDPxupfo

### Specifications
- OG: 1.047 | FG: 1.011 | ABV: ~4.7% | IBU: 10
- Target pH: 5.2 | Carbonation: 3.0-3.2 vol CO2

### Grain Bill
- 50-70% Wheat Malt + Pilsner/Pale Malt
- Rice hulls (prevent stuck mash)

### Hops
- Czech Saaz - single addition at 60-min boil start (10 IBU)

### Yeast
- Fermentis SafAle T-58 (ferment 18-22°C typical)

### Fruit Addition
- Pineapple Puree: 180 kg total (90 kg + 90 kg)
- Added post-fermentation under CO2 blanket (7 PSI)
- CO2 blown through carb stone to mix

### Mash Schedule
- Ferulic Acid Rest: 43-45°C for 15 min (clove phenols)
- HERMS system for main mash

### Process
1. 60-min boil
2. 15-min whirlpool stand
3. 12 days fermentation to terminal gravity
4. Fruit addition under CO2 purge
5. Carbonate: 12 PSI at 0.5°C (5.4g CO2/L)

### Tasting Notes
Massive clove/banana aroma, thick mouthfeel, dominant pineapple finish
