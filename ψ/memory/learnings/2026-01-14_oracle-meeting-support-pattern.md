# Oracle as Meeting Support Tool

## The Pattern

Oracle traces can provide real-time talking points during live meetings.

## What Worked

1. **/trace [topic]** surfaces relevant philosophy and learnings
2. Quick responses enable on-the-fly research during discussions
3. Historical data (/hours) adds concrete evidence to claims

## Key Discovery

Found while supporting Bitkub meeting discussion:

> **"The Oracle Keeps the Human Human" is about FREEDOM, not money.**
> Money is just one example of obstacle to freedom.

This learning (from 2026-01-03) was exactly what the conversation needed.

## The FREEDOM Chain

```
AI removes obstacles → Human has freedom → Human connects with humans
                    ↓
              Not about productivity
              Not about money
              About FREEDOM to be human
```

## Meeting Support Flow

```
Meeting discussion → /trace [topic] → Relevant insight → Share with team
     ↓                   ↓                  ↓
  Real-time          30-60 seconds      On-topic ammunition
```

## Anti-Pattern

Don't:
- Run complex technical operations during meetings
- Expect /watch (Gemini) to work with interruptions
- Try to do deep coding while supporting conversations
