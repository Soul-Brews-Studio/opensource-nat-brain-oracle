## Sadhu Framework - Competitive Research (Jan 2026)

**What**: n8n automation course for Thai Buddhist content creators
**Price**: ฿2,900 (Early Bird) - one-time
**Results**: เณรน้อย พอดแคสต์ - 18.9M views, 88K followers in 8 months

### Business Model
- Pre-built workflow templates
- Self-paced video course
- Community support included
- Updates for 2026 included

### What They Do Well
- Clear case study with metrics
- Emotional messaging ("get your life back")
- One-time pricing (no subscription)
- Thai-first approach
- Low price point for market

### Their Weakness (Our Opportunity)
- Template-based = everyone has same output
- Platform-dependent (n8n locked)
- Can't modify without understanding
- Need to buy new course for new problems
- Single niche (Buddhist content only)

### Our Counter-Position
- Skill-based not template-based
- "สอนตกปลา ไม่ใช่ให้ปลา"
- Custom solutions for YOUR problems
- Transferable skills to any tool
- Live workshop = real-time help
