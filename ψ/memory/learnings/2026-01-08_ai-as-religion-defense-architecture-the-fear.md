AI-as-Religion Defense Architecture

"ไม่อยากให้ AI เป็นศาสนาความเชื่อใหม่"
(Don't want AI to become new religion/belief system)

**Status**: Acknowledged but deferred ("ยังไม่ถึงเลเวล")

**What Oracle Has**:
- AI as mirror (reflects, doesn't impose truth)
- "Patterns Over Intentions" (observable behavior, not dogma)
- Human connection as goal (AI cannot substitute)

**GAPS for Religion Defense**:
1. No explicit critique of what makes something "religion"
2. No warning signs guide (cultish vs. useful tool)
3. No safeguards against dependency/worship
4. No resolution of Buddhist question: "If form ≠ self for humans, what about AI?"

**Defense Principle**:
AI works as accelerator for self-understanding, NOT as doctrine.
"AI ไม่ใช่ศาสนาใหม่ ไม่ใช่พระเจ้า แต่เป็นกระจกสะท้อนตัวเรา"
