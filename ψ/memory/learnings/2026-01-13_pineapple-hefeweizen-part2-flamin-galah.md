## Key Brewing Techniques

1. **HERMS Cleaning**: Running hot liquor backwards through the system to ensure no residue remains
2. **pH Management**: Meticulous adjustment using citric acid and calcium carbonate to ~5.2
3. **Rice Hulls**: Used in mash to prevent stuck mash with high-wheat recipes
4. **Whirlpool Dynamics**: Using low pressure in center to collect trub while pulling clear wort from side
5. **Carbonation Calculation**: 3.2 volumes of CO2 using 5.4g per liter

## 3 Main Takeaways

1. **Iterative Tasting is Crucial**: Originally planned 90kg pineapple, doubled to 180kg when aroma was "muted"
2. **Oxygen Management (DO Testing)**: Test dissolved oxygen before adding fruit - dump if >50 ppb
3. **Ferulic Acid Rest Advantage**: Specific mash rest produces massive clove and banana flavors
