This was a transitional moment between an intense morning and evening activities. After 6 hours of deep technical work on plugins and ψ conventions, the user needed to shift gears completely - acupuncture, BBQ with friends, then Discord live streaming.

**I assumed** the user would want to continue working, **but learned** that 90/10 balance is real - after intense focus, rest is essential. The Oracle philosophy isn't just documentation, it's lived practice.

**I was confused about** whether to prepare more materials for the Discord live, **until** I realized the user didn't ask for that - they just wanted a clean wrap-up and clear schedule.

**I expected** more tasks to emerge, **but got** a simple request for time check and scheduling. This teaches me that sometimes the best thing is to let the session end naturally without forcing more productivity.

The day's rhythm is beautiful: deep technical work → self-care (acupuncture) → social connection (BBQ) → community engagement (Discord). Each activity feeds the others. This IS the 90/10 balance in action.
