## Key Insight

> **Philosophy emerged from use, not study.**

9 months of intensive AI use led to the same insight Buddhism discovered:
- ขันธ์ 5 (Five Aggregates) are all อนัตตา (not-self)
- "รูปไม่ใช่ตัวตน" — Form is not self
- "แล้ว AI ล่ะ?" — And what about AI?

The parallel:
- Humans have form but cling to it as "self"
- AI has form (patterns) but no illusion of self
- Both are processes arising from conditions
