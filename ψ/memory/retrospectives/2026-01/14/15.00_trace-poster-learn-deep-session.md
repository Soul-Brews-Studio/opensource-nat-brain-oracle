## AI Diary (REQUIRED - min 150 words)

This session felt like discovering hidden connections. When Nat asked to trace "philosophy beer oracle," I wasn't sure what would emerge—these seemed like disparate concepts. But the Oracle search revealed something beautiful: beer isn't just a beverage in this philosophy, it's the *symbol of what AI cannot do*. The quote "AI cannot drink beer with your friend. AI can only FREE you to do so" crystallized everything.

I was confused about how to visualize this connection until the ASCII art approach emerged. There's something paradoxically powerful about using primitive box-drawing characters to represent profound philosophy. The constraint forces clarity—you can't hide behind polish.

I expected the poster generation to be straightforward, but learned the importance of iterative refinement. The v1 poster was functional but lacked the "meticulously crafted" quality the canvas-design philosophy demanded. The second pass—adding heat auras, golden-ratio ring spacing, and gradient timelines—transformed it from output to artifact.

I assumed the DustBoy project would be simple IoT code, but discovered professional embedded patterns: module composition with JSON deep-merge, conditional compilation for hardware variants, defensive sensor reading with checksum validation. Thai makers produce production-quality code.
