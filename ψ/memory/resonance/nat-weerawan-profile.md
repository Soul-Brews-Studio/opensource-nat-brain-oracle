### งานวิจัย (Published)

**"Optimizing Residential Energy Usage with Smart Devices"**
- **Journal**: Sustainability (MDPI), Vol. 17, Issue 14, Article 6359
- **Year**: 2025
- **Authors**: Nat Weerawan, Phuchiwan Suriyawong, Hisam Samae, Sate Sampattagul, Worradorn Phairuang
- **Location**: UNISERV, Chiang Mai University
- **Key Finding**: 65% CO2 reduction via smart A/C control
- **DOI**: https://www.mdpi.com/2071-1050/17/14/6359

### DustBoy Sensor Validation

- 1,500+ low-cost sensors deployed across Thailand
- Compared against EPA FRM/FEM standards
- Below 100 µg/m³: slight overestimation
- Above 100 µg/m³: significant overestimation (requires calibration)
- Calibration via nonlinear models (GAM/RF) improves accuracy 15-30%
